If you're a Resident in the UAE:

If you're a resident of the UAE and wish to travel to another country, UAE residency allows you to travel to some countries and obtain a visa on arrival. Visa requirements depending on the country you intend to travel to. It's recommended to check the visa requirements for your intended destination well ahead of your travel date to ensure you have all the necessary documents. You can verify travel requirements by reaching out to our travel consultants.
