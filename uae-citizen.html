If you're a UAE Citizen:

As a UAE citizen, your UAE passport allows you to travel visa-free or obtain a visa on arrival to many countries around the world. This is based on existing diplomatic and trade relations. However, there might be exceptions and varying conditions from one country to another. To get accurate information, you can check with our travel consultants.
